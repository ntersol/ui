Custom Footer

Introducing a customizable footer for the form generator that allows you to add custom content and tailor the footer to your specific needs. With the footerContentLeft and footerContentRight directives, you can add custom content to the left and right of the footer, respectively, including replacing the default submit button with your own custom button. If you choose not to replace the submit button, you can use the beforeSubmitButton and afterSubmitButton directives to insert custom content before or after the submit button. With these customizable footer options, you can create forms that look and feel exactly the way you want them to, providing a seamless user experience and making it easy to collect the data you need.

Available Directives:

  • footerContentLeft: Directly places custom content into the left of the footer.
  • footerContentRight: Replaces the content on the right of the footer, including the submit button, with custom content.
  • beforeSubmitButton: Inserts custom content before the default submit button in the footer.
  • afterSubmitButton: Inserts custom content after the default submit button in the footer.
