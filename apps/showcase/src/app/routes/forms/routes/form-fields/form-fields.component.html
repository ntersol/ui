Form Fields

This is a library designed to make building and managing forms quicker and more consistent. Its key features include:

  • Streamlined design to speed up the process of creating and managing forms
  • Consistent support and styling for standard form elements such as labels, placeholders, and hints
  • Ability to create forms and fields from models
  • Improved error handling with on-blur validation for initial errors and real-time validation after
  • Support for advanced validators, allowing for more precise and customized validation of form inputs
  • A wide range of form controls, including zip code, phone number, and currency inputs, all with self contained business logic and validation.
